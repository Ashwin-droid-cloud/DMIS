
ResQNet
Real-Time Response Coordination System
ResQNet is a coordination and response intelligence system designed to support decision-making in time-critical situations. It provides a unified operational view by connecting live inputs, field activity, and resource availability into a single, structured workflow.
Problem
Operational response often suffers from fragmented information, delayed communication, and inefficient resource use. Teams act on partial visibility, leading to slower and less effective outcomes.
Approach
ResQNet introduces a centralized coordination layer that integrates incoming data, prioritizes events, and supports consistent decision-making across teams. The system focuses on reducing ambiguity and enabling faster, more aligned responses.
Capabilities
Situation Tracking
Continuous monitoring of evolving events through a unified interface
Resource Coordination
Allocation of personnel and assets based on priority and availability
Operational Alignment
Shared view across teams to reduce duplication and miscommunication
Geospatial Context
Visualization of events and resources for better situational awareness
